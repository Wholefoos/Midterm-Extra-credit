Escape Reality

Life is really stressful at times due to work, school, or any other problems. When I am not dealing with any of those problems, playing videogames helps you temorarily escape and get lost in a new world without those problems. There are so many types of games available with so many stories that everyone can find at least one game that they enjoy and get lost in it.

Play with friends

Another way to play videogames is to play with friends. If the game can be played online then you can have friends or a random people play along-side you or against you. Personally these games are really enjoyable because they allow you to have have fun and spend time with friends no matter how far they live from you.

Have fun

Last but not least, Have Fun! Games can get really competetive or difficult at times but you must always remember to have fun because you are using YOUR time, your time should always be enjoyed when gaming
